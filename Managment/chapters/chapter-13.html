التوظيف — Recruitment

جذب الموظفين المحتملين وتصفيتهم للوصول للمجموعة اللي هنختار منها.

🧲 Definition & Purpose
Definition
Is the initial attraction and screening of the supply of prospective human resources available to fill a position.
Recruitment مش معناها اختيار نهائي. هي بس تجيب candidates وتفلترهم مبدئياً لحد ما يبقى عندك pool مناسب للـ Selection.
Purpose
To narrow a large field of prospective employees to a relatively small group from which someone will be hired.
يعني نقلل العدد الكبير لمجموعة صغيرة نقدر نختار منها.

🔍 1. Knowing The Job (Job Analysis)

علشان نعين صح، لازم نفهم الوظيفة عن طريق Job Analysis واللي بتطلع لنا حاجتين:

Job Description (وصف الوظيفة)

A list of specific activities that must be performed by whoever holds the position.

Activities / المهام

Job Specification (مواصفات الشخص)

A list of specific characteristics of the individual who should be hired for the job.

Characteristics / المهارات والصفات
فخ متكرر: لو الاختيار بيتكلم عن job responsibilities / duties / activities يبقى Job Description. لو بيتكلم عن physical skills / qualifications / experience / characteristics يبقى Job Specification.
🏢 2 & 3. Sources Inside the Organization

بعض الأحيان الترقية من الداخل (Promotion from within) بتكون أفضل. مميزاتها:

  • Building employee morale. (بترفع الروح المعنوية)
  • Encouraging employees to work harder.
  • Attracting employees to stay with the organization.

Human Resource Inventory

Information about the characteristics of organization members (Past Performance and Future Potential).
Objective: To keep management up to date about the possibilities for filling a position from within.

Wikstrom Human Resource Inventory

Wikstrom قال إن فيه 3 أنواع من السجلات (Records):

1. Management Inventory Card

Contains an employee organizational history and an indication of how he might be used in the future.

2. Position Replacement Form

Focuses on position-centered information rather than people-centered information.

3. Management Manpower Replacement Chart

A composite view of the individuals management considers significant for HR planning.

مهم جداً: نظام Wikstrom هو الأساس لعملية الـ Succession planning (التخطيط لمن سيخلف من في المناصب).
Succession planning: عملية تحديد مين هيخلف مين في المناصب التنظيمية. والـ software ممكن يساعد المديرين يحتفظوا بمخزون HR معقد ويقرروا أفضل deployment/development للموظفين.
🌍 4. Sources Outside the Organization

Competitors

مصدر قوي لأن الشخص عارف المجال، والمنافس دفع جزء من تكلفة التدريب، وكمان ممكن تضعف المنافس وتعرف أكثر عن طريقة منافسته.

Employment Agencies

بتعمل matching بين الناس والوظائف. ممكن تكون Public أو Private.

Readers of Certain Publications

إعلان في publication مناسب حسب مستوى الوظيفة ونوع المرشحين المطلوبين.

Educational Institutions

مقابلة طلاب قريبين من التخرج، خصوصاً لو المنظمة محتاجة entry-level talent.

⚖️ 5 & 6. Knowing the Law & Affirmative Action
Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC)
Enforce federal laws prohibiting discrimination on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, and national origin in recruitment, hiring, firing, layoffs, and all other employment practices.
السلايدز كمان بتذكر Pregnancy Discrimination, Age Discrimination, Disability Discrimination.
Affirmative Action Program
To eliminate barriers and increase opportunities for underutilized and/or disadvantaged individuals.

How to measure progress?

  • Count employed minority/disadvantaged individuals.
  • Compare with how many should be employed according to EEOC guidelines.
  • Compare the two numbers and judge the gap.

Why modern managers care?

مش بس بسبب القانون. السلايدز بتقول إن labor supply اتغير وفيه diversity في minorities, immigrants, women, age groups, and work values.

الاختيار — Selection

اختيار الفرد المناسب للتعيين من ضمن الناس اللي قدموا.

🎯 Definition & Tools
Selection
Choosing an individual to hire from all those who have been recruited.
Selection dependent on Recruitment. كل stage بتقلل العدد لحد ما يتعين شخص واحد.

Selection Process Stages

StageReason for elimination
Preliminary screening from records/data sheetsLack of adequate educational and performance record
Preliminary interviewObvious misfit from outward appearance and conduct
Intelligence tests / Aptitude testsFailure to meet minimum standards or aptitude
Personality tests / Performance referencesNegative personality aspects or negative past-performance reports
Diagnostic interviewLack of necessary innate ability, ambition, or other qualities
Physical examination / Personal judgmentPhysically unfit; remaining candidate placed in available position

Selection Tools: Testing

Aptitude Tests

Measure the potential to perform a task (Intelligence, Special Abilities like mechanical/clerical).

Achievement Tests

Measure the level of skill or knowledge (e.g., Typing tests).

Vocational Interest Tests

Measure interest in performing various kinds of jobs.

Personality Tests

Describe personality dimensions (emotional maturity, honesty).

Selection Tools: Assessment Centers

A program (not a place) where participants engage in individual and group exercises (like leaderless discussions or role-playing) simulated for important activities.
Trained assessors (Managers) observe and assess skills like communication, conflict resolution, leadership.

Exercises may include

  • Leaderless discussions.
  • Oral presentations.
  • Leading a group to solve an assigned problem.

Assessment criteria

  • Oral communication.
  • Conflict resolution.
  • Leadership, persuasion, and problem solving.
05

التدريب — Training

تطوير مهارات الموظفين لزيادة إنتاجيتهم وتحقيق أهداف المنظمة.

Training Definition
The process of developing qualities in human resources that will enable them to be more productive and thus contribute more to organizational goal attainment.
Purpose: increase employee productivity by influencing their behavior.
1
Determining Training Needs
Evaluation of production process, direct feedback from employees, or looking into the future.
2
Designing the Training Program
Assembling facts and activities to meet the established needs.
3
Administering the Training Program
Transmitting information & Developing skills.
4
Evaluating the Training Program
Determine whether it meets the needs for which it was designed.
Training Needs: هي information أو skill areas عند فرد/مجموعة محتاجة تطوير علشان الإنتاجية تزيد. طرق تحديدها: تقييم production process، feedback مباشر من الموظفين، والنظر للمستقبل/current and projected changes.

Techniques for Transmitting Information

Lectures

One-way communication where instructor orally presents information to listeners.

  • Advantage: maximum amount of information in limited time.
  • Disadvantages: little chance to clarify meanings, check understanding, or practice.
  • Minimal value for attitudinal or behavioral change.

Programmed Learning

Instruction without a human instructor. Information is divided into small parts and trainees check their own answers.

  • Can be computerized.
  • Students learn at their own pace.
  • Immediate right/wrong feedback.
  • Weakness: no one answers confused learner questions.

Techniques for Developing Skills

On-The-Job

  • Coaching: Direct critiquing of performance.
  • Position Rotation: Moving from job to job to understand the whole org.
  • Special Project Committees: Assigning tasks to furnish experience.

ده كله بيخلط job-related knowledge مع real experience.

Classroom

  • Management Games: Small groups evaluate management decisions.
  • Role-playing: Acting out to solve people-oriented problems.

مفيد لما عايز simulation قبل ما الشخص يواجه الموقف الحقيقي.

Evaluating the Training Program: الإدارة لازم تشوف البرنامج حقق الاحتياجات اللي اتصمم لها ولا لأ. لو مش محقق، يبقى محتاج تغييرات مهمة.

تقييم الأداء — Performance Appraisal

مراجعة أداء الموظفين وتقديم feedback لزيادة إنتاجيتهم.

Performance Appraisal Definition
The process of reviewing individuals' past productive activity to evaluate the contribution they have made toward attaining management system objectives.
Purpose: furnish feedback to organization members so they can become more productive and useful.
📈 Why Use It?
  • Support salary increases, promotions, or terminations.
  • Tell subordinates how they are doing (where they stand with the boss).
  • Furnish a useful basis for coaching.

How to Handle Performance Appraisals

Focus on position performance

Stress performance in the position the individual holds and success in attaining organizational objectives.

Judge job performance

Emphasize how well the person is doing the job, not evaluator's vague impression of work habits.

Acceptable process

The appraisal should be acceptable to both the evaluator and the subject.

Potential Weaknesses

Weaknesses to Avoid:
  • Focusing on short-term rewards instead of long-run success.
  • Viewing it as a reward-punishment situation.
  • Emphasis on completing paperwork rather than critiquing performance.
  • Viewed as unfair or biased.
  • Subordinates react negatively when evaluators offer unfavorable comments.
How to Avoid: Treat appraisal as an opportunity to increase employee worth through constructive feedback, not reward/punishment. Paperwork is only an aid, and feedback should be tactful and objective.
