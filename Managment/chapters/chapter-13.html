Definitions — التعريفات الأساسية

أول ما تدخل الشابتر، لازم تعرف الفرق بين الـ Appropriate وغيره، وإيه معنى Valuable Contribution.

📖 المصطلحات الأساسية — Key Definitions
Appropriate Human Resources
The individuals within the organization who make a valuable contribution to management system goal attainment.
يعني: الأشخاص اللي بيساهموا بشكل فعّال في تحقيق أهداف المنظمة.
Inappropriate Human Resources
The individuals within the organization who don't make a valuable contribution to management system goal attainment.
يعني: اللي مش بيساهموا بشكل فعّال — هدف الـ HRM إنهم يتحوّلوا لـ Appropriate أو يتم استبدالهم.
Valuable Contribution
Could be determined by how human resources interact and cooperate to use all other management system resources.
مش بس إنه بيشتغل — المهم إزاي بيتعاون مع غيره ويستخدم كل الموارد المتاحة بكفاءة.

جذب الموظفين المحتملين وتصفيتهم للوصول للمجموعة اللي هنختار منها.

🧲 Definition & Purpose
Definition
Is the initial attraction and screening of the supply of prospective human resources available to fill a position.
Recruitment مش معناها اختيار نهائي. هي بس تجيب candidates وتفلترهم مبدئياً لحد ما يبقى عندك pool مناسب للـ Selection.
Purpose
To narrow a large field of prospective employees to a relatively small group from which someone will be hired.
يعني نقلل العدد الكبير لمجموعة صغيرة نقدر نختار منها.

🏢 2 & 3. Sources Inside the Organization

بعض الأحيان الترقية من الداخل (Promotion from within) بتكون أفضل. مميزاتها:

  • Building employee morale. (بترفع الروح المعنوية)
  • Encouraging employees to work harder.
  • Attracting employees to stay with the organization.

Human Resource Inventory

Information about the characteristics of organization members (Past Performance and Future Potential).
Objective: To keep management up to date about the possibilities for filling a position from within.

Wikstrom Human Resource Inventory

Wikstrom قال إن فيه 3 أنواع من السجلات (Records):

1. Management Inventory Card

Contains an employee organizational history and an indication of how he might be used in the future.

2. Position Replacement Form

Focuses on position-centered information rather than people-centered information.

3. Management Manpower Replacement Chart

A composite view of the individuals management considers significant for HR planning.

مهم جداً: نظام Wikstrom هو الأساس لعملية الـ Succession planning (التخطيط لمن سيخلف من في المناصب).

Knowing the Job — معرفة الوظيفة

الخطوة الأولى في الـ Recruitment — Job Analysis → Job Description + Job Specification.

🔍 Job Analysis & Its Output
Job Analysis
Is a technique commonly used to gain understanding of the job with the aim of determining a job description and a job specification.
أداة لفهم الوظيفة — بتطلع منها حاجتين: وصف الوظيفة (Description) + مواصفات الشخص (Specification).

📄 Job Description

A list of specific activities that must be performed by whoever holds the position.

بيوصف الوظيفة نفسها — إيه المهام والمسؤوليات.

  • Job title
  • Job summary
  • Working conditions
  • Equipment used
  • Job responsibilities

🧑 Job Specification

A list of specific characteristics of the individual who should be hired for the job.

بيوصف الشخص المطلوب — إيه المهارات والمواصفات.

  • Physical skills
  • Educational qualifications
  • Experience required
  • Personal qualities

التدريب — Training

تطوير مهارات الموظفين لزيادة إنتاجيتهم وتحقيق أهداف المنظمة.

Training Definition
The process of developing qualities in human resources that will enable them to be more productive and thus contribute more to organizational goal attainment.
Purpose: increase employee productivity by influencing their behavior.
1
Determining Training Needs
Evaluation of production process, direct feedback from employees, or looking into the future.
2
Designing the Training Program
Assembling facts and activities to meet the established needs.
3
Administering the Training Program
Transmitting information & Developing skills.
4
Evaluating the Training Program
Determine whether it meets the needs for which it was designed.
Training Needs: هي information أو skill areas عند فرد/مجموعة محتاجة تطوير علشان الإنتاجية تزيد. طرق تحديدها: تقييم production process، feedback مباشر من الموظفين، والنظر للمستقبل/current and projected changes.

Techniques for Transmitting Information

Lectures

One-way communication where instructor orally presents information to listeners.

  • Advantage: maximum amount of information in limited time.
  • Disadvantages: little chance to clarify meanings, check understanding, or practice.
  • Minimal value for attitudinal or behavioral change.

Programmed Learning

Instruction without a human instructor. Information is divided into small parts and trainees check their own answers.

  • Can be computerized.
  • Students learn at their own pace.
  • Immediate right/wrong feedback.
  • Weakness: no one answers confused learner questions.

Techniques for Developing Skills

On-The-Job

  • Coaching: Direct critiquing of performance.
  • Position Rotation: Moving from job to job to understand the whole org.
  • Special Project Committees: Assigning tasks to furnish experience.

ده كله بيخلط job-related knowledge مع real experience.

Classroom

  • Management Games: Small groups evaluate management decisions.
  • Role-playing: Acting out to solve people-oriented problems.

مفيد لما عايز simulation قبل ما الشخص يواجه الموقف الحقيقي.

Evaluating the Training Program: الإدارة لازم تشوف البرنامج حقق الاحتياجات اللي اتصمم لها ولا لأ. لو مش محقق، يبقى محتاج تغييرات مهمة.

⚖️ EEOC — Equal Employment Opportunity Commission
EEOC
Enforces federal laws prohibiting discrimination on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, and national origin in recruitment, hiring, firing, layoffs, and all other employment practices.
هيئة حكومية بتطبّق القوانين الفيدرالية ضد التمييز في كل مراحل التوظيف.
=======
Performance Appraisal Definition
The process of reviewing individuals' past productive activity to evaluate the contribution they have made toward attaining management system objectives.
Purpose: furnish feedback to organization members so they can become more productive and useful.
📈 Why Use It?
  • Support salary increases, promotions, or terminations.
  • Tell subordinates how they are doing (where they stand with the boss).
  • Furnish a useful basis for coaching.

How to Handle Performance Appraisals

Focus on position performance

Stress performance in the position the individual holds and success in attaining organizational objectives.

Judge job performance

Emphasize how well the person is doing the job, not evaluator's vague impression of work habits.

Acceptable process

The appraisal should be acceptable to both the evaluator and the subject.

Potential Weaknesses

Weaknesses to Avoid:
  • Focusing on short-term rewards instead of long-run success.
  • Viewing it as a reward-punishment situation.
  • Emphasis on completing paperwork rather than critiquing performance.
  • Viewed as unfair or biased.
  • Subordinates react negatively when evaluators offer unfavorable comments.

Selection — الاختيار

بعد الـ Recruitment، بيجي الـ Selection — اختيار شخص واحد من بين المرشحين المتبقيين.

🎯 Definition
Selection — Definition
Choosing an individual to hire from all those who have been recruited.
كل مرحلة في الـ Selection بتقلّل المجموعة لحد ما يتوظف شخص واحد. وهي دايماً dependent on the recruitment step.
📋 Selection Process Steps
Stage Reason for Elimination
Preliminary screening (records, data sheets) Lack of adequate educational and performance record
Preliminary interview Obvious misfit from outward appearance and conduct
Intelligence tests, Aptitude tests Failure to meet minimum standards / minimum necessary aptitude
Personality tests, Performance references Negative aspects of personality / Unfavorable past performance reports
Diagnostic interview Lack of necessary innate ability, ambition, or other qualities
Physical examination Physically unfit for job
Personal judgment Remaining candidate placed in available position ✅
🧰 Selection Tools — أدوات الاختيار

🧪 Testing

Examining human resources for qualities relevant to performing available jobs.

  • Aptitude Tests: Measure potential (General Intelligence, Mechanical, Clerical, Visual)
  • Achievement Tests: Measure current skill level (e.g., Typing Tests)
  • Vocational Interest Tests: Measure interest in various jobs
  • Personality Tests: Describe personality dimensions (emotional maturity, honesty, objectivity)

🏢 Assessment Centers

A program (not a place!) in which participants engage in individual and group exercises to simulate important activities at levels they aspire to.

  • Leaderless discussions
  • Oral presentations
  • Group problem solving
  • Trained Assessors observe exercises

Assessment criteria: oral communication, conflict resolution, leadership, persuasion, problem solving.

⚠️ نقطة مهمة عن Assessment Centers: هي program مش place! كتير من الطلاب بيعتقدوا إنها مكان، لكن التعريف الصح: "A program in which participants engage in exercises..."

Training — التدريب

الخطوة الثالثة بعد ما الموظف ينضم — تطوير مهاراته وزيادة إنتاجيته.

📚 Definition & Purpose
Training — Definition
The process of developing qualities in human resources that will enable them to be more productive and thus contribute more to organizational goal attainment.
يعني: تطوير الكفاءات اللي هتزوّد إنتاجية الموظف وإسهامه في تحقيق أهداف المنظمة.
Training — Purpose
To increase the productivity of employees by influencing their behavior.
الهدف مش بس التعليم — الهدف تغيير السلوك بما يزيد الإنتاجية.
🔄 4 Steps of Training — الخطوات الأربعة (سؤال امتحان 2019!)

✅ سؤال امتحان 2019: "Complete the missing training steps" — الإجابة: Step 2 = Design program, Step 3 = Administrate program, Step 4 = Evaluate.
🔎 Step 1: Determining Training Needs
Training Needs
The information or skill areas of an individual/group that need further development to increase the productivity of that individual/group.

طرق تحديد الاحتياجات التدريبية:

  • Evaluating the production process within the organization
  • Getting direct feedback from employees on what they believe are training needs
  • Looking into the future — إيه اللي المنظمة هتحتاجه مستقبلاً
✅ سؤال امتحان 2025 (Final): "Which is a method used in group or organizational training needs assessment?" — الإجابة: Consideration of current and projected changes.
🎓 Step 3: Administering the Training Program

دلوقتي بنوصل للتنفيذ الفعلي — في 4 تقنيات أساسية:

Techniques for Transmitting Information

📢 Lectures

One-way communication — مدرس بيشرح لمجموعة مستمعين.

  • Advantage Exposes trainees to maximum information in short time
  • Disadvantage No chance to clarify, check understanding, or practice
  • Disadvantage Minimal value in changing attitudes or behavior

💻 Programmed Learning

التعليم بدون مدرس بشري — المتدرب بيجاوب ويعرف صح أو غلط فوراً.

  • Advantage Learn at own pace
  • Advantage Immediate feedback (right or wrong)
  • Advantage Active participation
  • Disadvantage No one to answer confused learner's questions

Techniques for Developing Skills

🏭 On-The-Job

  • Coaching: Direct critiquing of how well an individual is performing
  • Position Rotation: Moving from job to job to understand the whole organization
  • Special Project Committees: Assigning a particular task to gain experience in a designated area

🏫 Classroom

  • Management Games: Small groups make and evaluate management decisions
  • Role-playing Format: Acting out people-oriented problems that must be solved

Performance Appraisal — تقييم الأداء

الخطوة الأخيرة — مراجعة الأداء السابق لتحسين الإنتاجية المستقبلية.

📊 Definition & Purpose
Performance Appraisal (Review / Evaluation) — Definition
The process of reviewing individuals' past productive activity to evaluate the contribution they have made toward attaining management system objectives.
يعني: مراجعة الأداء الماضي — مش العقاب أو المكافأة، لكن التقييم الموضوعي.
Performance Appraisal — Purpose
To furnish feedback to organization members about how they can become more productive and useful to the organization in its quest for quality.
الهدف الأساسي = Feedback لتحسين الإنتاجية، مش مجرد ورق أو حكم على الموظف.
❓ Why Use Performance Appraisals?
①
Support HR Decisions
بتوفر أحكام منهجية لدعم salary increases, promotions, transfers, demotions or terminations.
②
Tell Subordinates How They're Doing
بتخلّي الموظفين يعرفوا مكانتهم ومطلوب منهم إيه — تغيير في behavior, attitudes, skills, or job knowledge.
③
Coaching and Counseling Basis
بتوفر basis مفيد للـ coaching and counseling بين المديرين والموظفين.
✅ Handling Performance Appraisals — كيف تُجرى صح
✅ المبادئ الصحيحة للتقييم
  • It should stress performance in the position — مش انطباع المقيّم عن الموظف.
  • It should emphasize how well the individual is doing the job — مش work habits.
  • It should be acceptable to both the evaluator and the subject.
⚠️ Potential Weaknesses & How to Avoid Them
❌ المشاكل المحتملة
  • تركّز الموظفين على الـ short-term rewards بدل أهداف المنظمة البعيدة.
  • الموظفون يشوفوها كـ reward-punishment situation.
  • التركيز على الأوراق (paperwork) بدل النقد الفعلي للأداء.
  • الموظف يشوف العملية غير عادلة أو متحيزة.
  • الموظف يرد بشكل سلبي على التعليقات غير المواتية.
✅ طرق تجنّب المشاكل
  • اشوف الـ appraisal كـ فرصة لتطوير الموظف، مش مكافأة/عقاب.
  • الـ paperwork وسيلة، مش هدف.
  • الـ feedback يكون tactful and objective قدر الإمكان.
⚠️ سؤال امتحان 2025: "Which performance appraisal method consumes a lot of time?" — الإجابة: Essay method (Final 2025 Q56). وكمان: "The actual achievements compared with objectives = Job Performance" (Q55).
⚠️ سؤال امتحان 2025: "Which term refers to evaluating and improving employee performance?" — الإجابة: Performance Appraisal (Final 2025 Q49).
