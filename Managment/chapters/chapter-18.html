Groups

01

Group Definition — تعريف الـ Group

مش أي تجمع ناس يبقى Group بالمعنى الإداري؛ لازم تتحقق 3 شروط.

👥 Official Definition
Group
Any number of people who interact with one another, are psychologically aware of one another, and perceive themselves to be a group.
بالعربي: ناس بينهم تفاعل، حاسين بوجود بعض كأعضاء، وشايفين نفسهم كـ group واحد.

1. Interact

لازم يكون فيه تواصل أو تعامل بين الأعضاء، مش مجرد ناس واقفة في نفس المكان.

2. Psychologically Aware

كل عضو مدرك نفسيًا إن فيه أعضاء تانيين موجودين معاه في نفس الـ group.

3. Perceive Themselves

الأعضاء شايفين نفسهم كيان واحد: "إحنا group".

ليه managers يدرسوا Groups؟ لأن Groups موجودة في كل أنواع المنظمات، وبتتكون طبيعيًا في كل أجزاء المنظمة، وممكن تسبب نتائج مرغوبة أو غير مرغوبة. فهمها بيرفع احتمال إن نتائجها تكون مفيدة للمنظمة.
02

Formal vs Informal Groups

السؤال هنا: الـ group اتعملت بقرار إداري ولا اتكونت طبيعيًا من العلاقات؟

Formal Group
A group that exists within an organization by virtue of management decree to perform tasks that enhance the attainment of organizational objectives.
يعني الإدارة هي اللي أنشأتها عشان شغل أو هدف تنظيمي.
Informal Group
A collection of individuals whose common work experiences result in the development of interpersonal relations beyond those established by management.
يعني علاقات اتكونت من التجربة المشتركة في الشغل، مش من قرار رسمي.
Point Formal Group Informal Group
Origin Management decree Common work experiences
Purpose Organizational objectives Social relations / shared concerns
Structure Official and planned Natural and unofficial
Examples Command Group, Task Group, Committee Interest Group, Friendship Group
اختصار امتحاني: لو السؤال بيقول formed by management أو designated work assignments غالبًا الإجابة Formal Group. لو بيقول common work experiences أو علاقات خارج الإدارة، الإجابة Informal Group.
03

Formal Group Types

السلايد بيقسم الـ Formal Groups إلى Command Groups و Task Groups.

Command Group

A formal group that is outlined in the chain of command on an organization chart.

بالعربي: مجموعة ظاهرة في الـ organization chart، زي manager وتحته supervisors. دي غالبًا routine وثابتة.

Task Group

A formal group of organization members who interact with one another to accomplish most of the organization’s nonroutine tasks.

بالعربي: مجموعة بتتعمل لمهمة غير روتينية أو مشكلة محددة. مش هي اللي في chain of command.
الغلط المتكرر: جملة "A task group is outlined in the chain of command on an organization chart" = False. ده تعريف Command Group مش Task Group.
04

Committees — اللجان

Committee غالبًا بتتصنف كـ Task Group.

Committee
A group of individuals charged with performing some type of specific activity and usually classified as a task group.
يعني لجنة معمولة لنشاط محدد، وغالبًا نوع من الـ Task Group.

Committees are established for four major reasons

  1. Exchange ideas بين أعضاء المنظمة.
  2. Generate suggestions and recommendations لوحدات تانية.
  3. Develop new ideas لحل مشاكل موجودة.
  4. Assist in policy development أو تطوير السياسات التنظيمية.

Why managers should use committees

  • Improve the quality of decision making.
  • Encourage honest opinions.
  • Increase participation in decision making.
  • Ensure representation of important groups in the decision-making process.
Committee Success Area What to remember
Procedural Steps Clear written goals, specified authority, optimum size, capable chairperson, permanent secretary, agenda before meeting, start/end on time.
People-Oriented Guidelines Rephrase ideas, bring all members into active participation, stimulate further thought.
Avoid Groupthink Don't let the desire for agreement kill realistic evaluation.
سؤال Final 2024: Specifying the authority of the committee يعتبر procedural step. أما bringing all members into active participation فهو people-oriented guideline.
05

Groupthink

واحدة من أكتر كلمات الشابتر ظهورًا في الامتحان.

Groupthink
The mode of thinking that group members engage in when the desire for agreement so dominates the group that it overrides the need to realistically appraise alternative problem solutions.
بالعربي: رغبة المجموعة في الاتفاق بتبقى أقوى من تقييم البدائل بواقعية، فالأعضاء يسكتوا اعتراضاتهم.

Symptoms

  • Member agrees publicly while privately opposing.
  • Members feel pressure to conform.
  • Members censor their own ideas.
  • Poor decisions and poor outcomes.

Exam Trap

الجملة اللي بتقول إن Groupthink هو منع النقد لحد ما كل الناس تعرض بدائلها غلط؛ ده أقرب لقاعدة في Brainstorming مش تعريف Groupthink.

Example: لو رئيس اللجنة بدأ بسؤال منحاز زي "Should we enter this foolish price war?" فهو بيدفع الناس لاتفاق معين بدل تقييم البدائل بموضوعية.
06

Teams vs Groups

كل Team هو Group، لكن مش كل Group هو Team.

Team
A group whose members influence one another toward the accomplishment of an organizational objective.
يعني فريق أعضاؤه بيأثروا في بعض لتحقيق هدف تنظيمي مشترك.
احفظها زي ما هي: Are all groups in organizations teams? No. Are all teams in organizations groups? Yes. Good managers constantly try to help groups become teams.
Feature Groups Teams
Goal Share information Collective performance
Synergy Neutral Positive
Skills Random and varied Complementary
Accountability Individual Individual and mutual
Roles & Responsibilities Not always explicitly defined or developed Clearly developed and discussed
Rules & Procedures Not formally developed; evolve according to group needs Clearly discussed and developed to help team work together
Methods Members interact and work is usually divided Members collaborate, coordinate efforts, and work interdependently
07

Team Development Stages

الترتيب مهم جدًا: Forming → Storming → Norming → Performing → Adjourning.

01

Forming

الأعضاء بيتعرفوا على بعض وعلى الهدف العام.

02

Storming

خلافات وصراع أدوار وآراء قبل الاستقرار.

03

Norming

تتكون قواعد وعلاقات وطريقة عمل مشتركة.

04

Performing

الفريق يشتغل بكفاءة ناحية الأداء والهدف.

05

Adjourning

إنهاء المهمة أو تفكيك الفريق بعد الإنجاز.

08

Informal Groups Types

أهم فرق: Interest Group مبنية على قضية، و Friendship Group مبنية على علاقة شخصية.

Interest Group

An informal group that gains and maintains membership primarily because of a common concern members have about a specific issue.

مثال امتحاني: A group of workers pressing management for better pay.

Friendship Group

An informal group that forms in organizations because of the personal affiliation members have with one another.

مثال: زملاء بقوا أصحاب بسبب قربهم أو اهتمامات شخصية مشتركة.

Benefits of Informal Group Membership

  • Perpetuation of social and cultural values that members consider important.
  • Status and social satisfaction that people might not enjoy without group membership.
  • Increased ease of communication among group members.
  • Increased desirability of the overall work environment.
09

Exam Add-on — Team Effectiveness

جزء ظهر في Final 2025 عن بناء فريق فعال، حتى لو مش واضح في ملف السلايدز المختصر.

Final 2025 Trap: Setting stable goals and priorities for the team مش People-related step. دي Organization-related step.

People-related steps

  • Make the team’s work satisfying.
  • Develop mutual trust.
  • Build good communication.
  • Minimize unresolved conflicts.
  • Deal with threats effectively.
  • Build job-security perception.

Organization-related steps

  • Build a stable overall organization structure.
  • Become involved in team events.
  • Reward and recognize teams.
  • Set stable goals and priorities for the team.

Task-related steps

  • Develop clear objectives and project plans.
  • Provide technical direction and leadership.
  • Establish autonomy and challenging work.
  • Appoint qualified personnel.
  • Encourage involvement.
  • Build visibility for team work.
Cross-functional team: A work team composed of people from different functional areas who are all focused on a specified objective.

TF-1 Final 2019 Q2f · Final 2021 Q21 · Final 2024 Q1-5 · Final 2025 Q1-9
"A task group is a formal group outlined in the chain of command on an organization chart."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False الجملة دي تعريف Command Group. أما Task Group فهو formal group لمهام nonroutine tasks.
TF-2 Final 2021 Q14 · Final 2024 Q1-6
"Groupthink is a group decision-making process in which negative feedback on any suggested alternative is forbidden until all members have presented alternatives."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False ده مش تعريف Groupthink. Groupthink = desire for agreement overrides realistic appraisal. الجملة أقرب لفكرة تأجيل النقد في Brainstorming.
TF-3 Final 2024 Q1-12
"The ideal number of committee members for most tasks seems to be from 15 to 20."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False السلايد بيقول the optimum size of the committee should be determined. يعني مفيش رقم ثابت 15-20، وكمان الرقم ده غالبًا كبير على معظم اللجان الفعالة.
TF-4 Final 2024 Q1-13
"An interest group is an informal group that forms in organizations because of the personal affiliation members have with one another."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False Personal affiliation = Friendship Group. Interest Group = common concern about a specific issue.
TF-5 Final 2025 Q1-10
"The membership of friendship groups in an organization tends to remain static over long periods of time."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False Friendship Groups بتتكون بسبب personal affiliation، والعضوية ممكن تتغير مع الوقت لما صداقات تنتهي أو صداقات جديدة تتكون.
TF-6 Final 2025 Q1-11
"A people-related step in building an effective team is setting stable goals and priorities for the team."
T True
F False
✅ الإجابة: False Setting stable goals and priorities = Organization-related step. الـ People-related steps تشمل mutual trust, good communication, conflict minimization, and job-security perception.

A transitory groups; recurrent groups
B in-groups; out-groups
C command groups; task groups

✅ الإجابة: C — command groups; task groups ده التقسيم الأساسي في السلايد: Formal Groups Kinds = Command Group و Task Group.

MCQ-2 Final 2019 Q1h
Which of the following is one of the characteristics of a group as used in management-related discussions?

E A group consists of members who are psychologically aware of one another.

✅ الإجابة: D — Groups invariably cause desirable consequences السلايد بيقول إن Groups can cause either desirable or undesirable consequences. كلمة invariably معناها دائمًا، ودي غلط.

MCQ-9 Final 2024 Q43
One of the benefits of utilizing committees is that _______.
A the decision-making process is much quicker than individual decision-making
B there is no limit on the size of the committee

E decision-makers don't have to represent the group they come from
✅ الإجابة: D — improve the quality of decision-making من أسباب استخدام Committees: تحسين جودة القرار، تشجيع الآراء الصريحة، زيادة المشاركة، وتمثيل مجموعات مهمة.
MCQ-10 Final 2024 Q46

A rephrasing ideas already expressed
B specifying the authority of the committee

✅ الإجابة: B — specifying the authority of the committee دي من Procedural Steps. الخيارات A/C/D من People-Oriented Guidelines.
MCQ-11 Final 2024 Q50

MCQ-12 Final 2024 Q52 · Final 2025 Q58
