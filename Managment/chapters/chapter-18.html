أساسيات الـ Group — التعريف والأهمية

يعني إيه Group وليه المديرين لازم يدرسوا المجموعات داخل المنظمات.

👥 تعريف الـ Group
Group
A gathering of people who interact with one another, are psychologically aware of one another, and perceive themselves to be a group.
لازم يكون فيه تفاعل + وعي نفسي + إحساس إننا مجموعة.
📌 لماذا يدرس المديرون المجموعات؟
  • Groups exist in all kinds of organizations.
  • Groups inevitably form in all facets of organizational existence.
  • Groups can cause desirable or undesirable consequences.
  • Understanding groups increases the chance of desirable outcomes.
02

Formal vs Informal Groups

فرق جوه المنظمة: مين اتعمل بقرار إداري ومين اتكوَّن طبيعيًا.

Formal Group
A group that exists by management decree to perform tasks that enhance organizational objectives.
بتتكوَّن بقرار إداري وتخدم أهداف المنظمة.
Informal Group
A collection of individuals whose common work experiences create interpersonal relations beyond management.
بتتكوَّن طبيعيًا بسبب علاقات وخبرات مشتركة.
ملاحظة: الـ Formal groups بتساعد في تحقيق الأهداف، والـ Informal groups ممكن تدعم أو تعيق الأداء حسب السلوك.
03

أنواع الـ Formal Groups

Command Groups, Task Groups, والـ Committees كنوع من الـ Task Groups.

Command Group

Formal group outlined in the chain of command on the organization chart.

روتيني + ثابت

Task Group

Formal group of members who interact to accomplish nonroutine tasks.

مهمة محددة + غير روتينية

Committees (نوع من Task Groups)

A committee is a group of individuals charged with performing a specific activity.

  • Exchange ideas.
  • Generate suggestions and recommendations.
  • Develop new ideas to solve problems.
  • Assist in developing organizational policies.
04

Committees & Groupthink

إزاي تخلي اللجنة ناجحة وتتجنب أخطر خطر: Groupthink.

✅ Why Use Committees?
  • Improve decision quality.
  • Encourage honest opinions.
  • Increase participation in decision making.
  • Ensure representation of important groups.

Procedural Steps

  • Goals clearly defined (preferably in writing).
  • Specify committee authority.
  • Determine optimum size.
  • Select chairperson based on ability.
  • Appoint permanent secretary.
  • Distribute agenda before meeting.
  • Start on time and announce ending time.

People-Oriented Guidelines

  • Rephrase ideas already expressed.
  • Bring all members into active participation.
  • Stimulate further thought by members.
Groupthink: A mode of thinking where the desire for agreement dominates and overrides realistic appraisal of alternatives. Members conform and self-censor, leading to poor decisions.
Example: A board meeting starts with a loaded question like “Should we enter this foolish price war?” — it biases the group toward agreement.
05

Teams vs Groups

كل Team هو Group، لكن مش كل Group هو Team.

Team
A group whose members influence one another toward the accomplishment of an organizational objective.
مدير كويس يحاول يحول الـ Groups إلى Teams.

Groups vs Teams (ملخص المقارنة)

Feature Groups Teams
Goal Share information Collective performance
Synergy Neutral Positive
Skills Random and varied Complementary
Accountability Individual Individual and mutual
Roles Not always defined Clearly discussed
Methods Work divided Interdependent collaboration
Team Building (People-Related): Setting stable goals and priorities helps align members and reduce conflict.
06

Team Development Stages

مراحل تطور الفريق من البداية إلى الإنهاء.

→

→

→

→
Adjourning
Before → Forming → Storming → Norming → Performing → Adjourning

Forming / Storming

تعرف الأعضاء على بعضهم ومحاولة التأثير على الأهداف والإجراءات.

Norming / Performing

ثقة وتعاون أعلى، ثم أداء جماعي لحل المشاكل وتحقيق الأهداف.

Adjourning

انتهاء المهمة وتفكيك الفريق المؤقت بعد إنجاز الهدف.

07

Informal Groups

أنواعها وفوائد العضوية فيها داخل المنظمة.

Interest Group

Gains and maintains membership primarily because of a common concern about a specific issue.

Friendship Group

Forms because of the personal affiliation members have with one another.

Benefits of Informal Group Membership

  • Perpetuation of social and cultural values.
  • Status and social satisfaction.
  • Increased ease of communication.
  • Increased desirability of the work environment.
