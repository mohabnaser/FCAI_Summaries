Role of Management (دور الإدارة)

Guiding the organization toward its goals through its resources (توجيه المنظمة نحو أهدافها من خلال مواردها)

🎯 The Manager's Role in Simple Terms (دور المدير ببساطة)

The Role of Management is to guide the organization toward achieving its goals through the optimal use of available resources. (دور الإدارة هو توجيه المنظمة نحو تحقيق أهدافها من خلال الاستخدام الأمثل للموارد المتاحة.)

📌 Simple Definition: The manager is the one who takes available resources (people, money, materials) and converts them into results that achieve the organization's goal. (المدير هو اللي بياخد الموارد المتاحة ويحولها لنتائج تحقق هدف المنظمة.)
02

Defining Management (تعريف الإدارة)

Management has more than one definition — let's look at the most important ones. (الإدارة ليها أكتر من تعريف — هنشوف الأهم)

📖 The Official Definition (التعريف الرسمي)

Management is the process of reaching organizational goals by working with and through people and other organizational resources.

(الإدارة هي عملية الوصول لأهداف المنظمة عن طريق العمل مع الناس ومن خلالهم، باستخدام الموارد المختلفة.)

💡 Three Perspectives (وجهات النظر الثلاثة)
  • Process: A series of recurring activities (سلسلة من الأنشطة المتكررة)
  • Knowledge: Specialized expertise in methods and approaches (معرفة متخصصة بالطرق والأساليب)
  • Individuals: The people who apply these activities (الناس اللي بتطبق هذه الأنشطة)
💡 Fun Note from the Slides: The book described the Manager humorously as: "a man or woman who is placed above you to use, abuse, reuse, and/or recycle you" — but of course that's NOT the official definition 😄 (ده مش التعريف الرسمي طبعاً 😄)
03

Management Functions (وظائف الإدارة الأربعة)

This is the most important topic in this chapter — it will definitely appear in the exam. (ده أهم موضوع في الشابتر ده — هييجي في الامتحان مضمون)

⭐ The 4 Functions in Order: Planning → Organizing → Influencing → Controlling (الوظائف الأربعة بالترتيب)
📋 1. Planning (التخطيط)

Planning = Determining what tasks need to be done, when, and how. (تحديد إيه المهام المطلوبة، إمتى، وإزاي تتعمل.)

What to do?

  • What tasks need to be executed? (إيه المهام المطلوب تنفيذها؟)
  • When should they be done? (إمتى تتنفّذ؟)
  • How should they be done? (إزاي تتنفّذ؟)

Common Mistakes: (الأخطاء الشائعة)

  • Not setting goals for all important areas (عدم وضع أهداف لكل المجالات المهمة)
  • Setting plans with high risks (وضع خطط محفوفة بمخاطر عالية)
  • Not exploring enough alternatives (عدم استكشاف بدائل كافية)
⚠️ Exam Trap: Planning is the first and primary function — NOT Organizing. (الـ Planning هو الوظيفة الأولى والأساسية — مش Organizing.)
🏗️ 2. Organizing (التنظيم)

Organizing = Assigning the planned tasks to departments and creating mechanisms for execution with the right people. (تعيين المهام المخططة للأقسام وإنشاء آليات تنفيذها وتعيين الناس المناسبة.)

What to do?

  • Assign planned tasks to departments (تعيين المهام المخططة للأقسام)
  • Create task execution mechanisms (إنشاء آليات تنفيذ المهام)
  • Assign the right people to the right work (تعيين العمل للناس المناسبة)

Common Mistakes:

  • Not creating departments properly (عدم إنشاء الأقسام بالشكل المناسب)
  • Not ensuring coordination between members (عدم التأكيد على التنسيق)
  • Creating inappropriate management spans (إنشاء نطاقات إدارة غير مناسبة)
🎭 3. Influencing (التأثير)

Influencing (also called: motivating, leading, directing, or actuating) = Guiding people's activities in the right direction and motivating them. (توجيه أنشطة الناس في الاتجاه المناسب وتحفيزهم.)

What to do?

  • Direct people's activities in the right direction (توجيه أنشطة الناس في الاتجاه الصح)
  • Motivate people (the largest part) (تحفيز الناس — الجزء الأكبر)

Common Mistakes:

  • Not communicating properly with members (عدم التواصل الصحيح مع الأعضاء)
  • Creating inappropriate communication networks (إنشاء شبكات تواصل غير مناسبة)
  • Being a manager but not a leader (أن تكون مدير بس مش قائد)
📌 Alternative names for Influencing: Motivating · Leading · Directing · Actuating — all the same function. (كلهم نفس الوظيفة.)
🔍 4. Controlling (الرقابة)

Controlling = Measuring the organization's performance and comparing it to planned standards, and applying corrective changes if needed. (قياس أداء المنظمة ومقارنته بالمعايير المخططة، وتطبيق تغييرات تحسينية لو محتاج.)

What to do?

  • Measure the organization's performance (قياس أداء المنظمة)
  • Compare it to planned standards (مقارنته بالمعايير المخططة)
  • Apply corrective changes if necessary (تطبيق تغييرات تحسينية لو لازم)

Common Mistakes:

  • Not following up on plan execution progress (عدم متابعة التقدم في تنفيذ الخطط)
  • Not setting appropriate performance standards (عدم تحديد معايير أداء مناسبة)
  • Not measuring performance to discover improvements (عدم قياس الأداء لاكتشاف التحسينات)
🔄 Interrelationship Between the 4 Functions (الترابط بين الوظائف الأربعة)

The 4 functions are NOT separate — they form a recurring cycle: (الوظائف الأربعة مش منفصلة — دايرة متكررة:)

Organizational Goals → Planning → Organizing → Influencing → Controlling → back to Goals
⚠️ Common Exam Question: "Of the four primary management functions, organizing usually precedes planning" → FALSE — Planning always comes first. (Planning أولاً دايماً.)
04

Organizational Resources (موارد المنظمة)

The inputs that the organization converts into products (المدخلات اللي بتحولها المنظمة لمنتجات)

👥 Human

Human resources — the people working in the organization (الموارد البشرية — الناس اللي بتشتغل في المنظمة)

💰 Monetary

Financial resources — money and funding (الموارد المالية — الفلوس والتمويل)

🏭 Raw Materials

Raw materials — physical inputs for production (المواد الخام — المدخلات المادية للإنتاج)

🖥️ Capital

Capital assets — machines and equipment (الأصول الرأسمالية — الآلات والمعدات)

🔄 The Production Process (عملية الإنتاج)
Resources (People, Money, Raw Materials, Capital) → Inputs → Production Process → Outputs → Finished Products (Goods & Services)
05

Effectiveness vs Efficiency (الفاعلية والكفاءة)

One of the most frequently tested concepts in exams (من أكتر المفاهيم اللي بتتسأل في الامتحانات)

🎯 Managerial Effectiveness (الفاعلية)

The degree to which organizational resources are used to achieve organizational goals. (مدى استخدام الموارد التنظيمية في تحقيق الأهداف.)

The closer the organization gets to its goals, the more effective the managers are. (كلما اقتربت المنظمة من أهدافها، كان المديرون أكثر فاعلية.)

Simple Formula: Effectiveness = Doing the RIGHT things (عمل الأشياء الصح)
⚡ Managerial Efficiency (الكفاءة)

The proportion of total resources that contributed to production. (نسبة الموارد الكلية اللي ساهمت في الإنتاج.)

The higher the proportion, the more efficient the manager. (كلما زادت النسبة، كان المدير أكثر كفاءة.)

Simple Formula: Efficiency = Doing things RIGHT (عمل الأشياء بالطريقة الصح)
📊 Effectiveness & Efficiency Matrix (مصفوفة الفاعلية والكفاءة)
Efficient ✅ (No resource waste) (ما هدرش موارد) Inefficient ❌ (Resource waste) (هدر موارد)
Effective ✅ (Reached goal) (وصل للهدف) ⭐ Best — reached goal without wasting resources (الأفضل) Reached goal but wasted resources (وصل للهدف بس هدر موارد)
Ineffective ❌ (Did not reach goal) (ما وصلش للهدف) Did not reach goal but did not waste resources (ما وصلش بس ما هدرش) ⛔ Worst — did not reach goal and wasted resources (الأسوأ)
⚠️ Exam Question: "The more resources unused during the production process, the more _______ the manager." → Inefficient (not Ineffective) (مش Ineffective)
06

Management Skills (مهارات المدير)

Classic View (Katz) vs Contemporary View (الرؤية الكلاسيكية والمعاصرة)

📚 Classic View — Robert L. Katz

Katz identified 3 core skills and linked them to management levels: (كاتز حدد 3 مهارات أساسية وربطها بمستويات الإدارة:)

🔧 Technical Skills

The ability to apply specialized knowledge in techniques and procedures (القدرة على تطبيق المعرفة المتخصصة في التقنيات والإجراءات)

→ Low-Level Managers

👥 Human Skills

The ability to work with people and build effective relationships (القدرة على العمل مع الناس وبناء علاقات فعالة)

→ Middle-Level Managers

🌐 Conceptual Skills

The ability to see the organization as a whole and understand how its parts interact (القدرة على رؤية المنظمة كوحدة متكاملة)

→ Top-Level Managers

⚠️ Common Exam Question: "Human skills, unlike technical skills, involve the ability to see the organization as a whole" → FALSE — that describes Conceptual Skills, not Human Skills. (ده وصف الـ Conceptual Skills مش Human.)
🚀 Contemporary View — 3 Activity Types (الأنشطة الثلاثة)

✅ Task-related

Managing organizational tasks — planning, monitoring, coordination (إدارة مهام المنظمة)

👥 People-related

Managing people — support, recognition, development, consulting (إدارة الناس)

🔄 Change-related

Managing change — modifying organizational components (إدارة التغيير)

Exam Question Bank — Chapter 1

Real questions from exams 2019 / 2021 / 2025 · Choose and try

Question 1 Final 2019 · Q1.b
The primary management function is _______.
A Organizing
B Planning
C Influencing
D Controlling
E Staffing
✅ Correct Answer: B — Planning Planning is the first and most important management function because everything else (Organizing, Influencing, Controlling) depends on it. You cannot organize something that hasn't been planned. (الـ Planning هو أول وأهم وظيفة إدارية لأن كل حاجة تانية بتعتمد عليه.)
Why others are wrong: A (Organizing) comes second. C and D are functions that come after Planning. E (Staffing) is not one of the four basic functions.
Question 2 Final 2021 · Q1.7
The affirmative purpose of planning is to minimize risk by reducing the uncertainties surrounding business conditions and clarifying the consequences of related management activities.
A True
B False
✅ Correct Answer: B — False This description is the role of the Protective purpose, not the Affirmative purpose. The Affirmative purpose is: to increase the degree of organizational success. The Protective purpose is what reduces risks. (الوصف ده هو دور الـ Protective purpose مش الـ Affirmative.)
Reason: The book distinguishes between two planning purposes — Protective (reducing risks) and Affirmative (increasing success).
Question 3 Final 2021 · Q1.1
Planning involves choosing tasks that must be performed to attain organizational goals, outlining how the tasks must be performed, and indicating when they should be performed.
A True
B False
✅ Correct Answer: A — True This is the exact definition of Planning — choosing tasks (What) + how to do them (How) + when to do them (When). (ده تعريف الـ Planning بالظبط.)
Question 4 Final 2021 · Q1.3
Influencing is the process of assigning the tasks developed under the planning function to various individuals or groups within the organization.
A True
B False
✅ Correct Answer: B — False This describes Organizing, not Influencing. Organizing is what distributes tasks to individuals and departments. Influencing is guiding and motivating people. (ده وصف الـ Organizing مش Influencing.)
Comparison: Organizing = task distribution · Influencing = guiding and motivating people
Question 5 Final 2021 · Q1.4
Organizing is also commonly referred to as motivating, leading, directing, or actuating.
A True
B False
✅ Correct Answer: B — False Motivating, Leading, Directing, Actuating — these are all alternative names for Influencing, not Organizing. (دي كلها أسماء بديلة للـ Influencing مش Organizing.)
Question 6 Final 2021 · Q1.6
Human skills, unlike technical skills, involve the ability to see the organization as a whole.
A True
B False
✅ Correct Answer: B — False Seeing the organization as a whole is the definition of Conceptual Skills, not Human Skills. Human Skills are the ability to work with people. (رؤية المنظمة كوحدة متكاملة هو تعريف الـ Conceptual Skills مش Human Skills.)
Question 7 Final 2021 · Q1.27
The more resources unused during the production process, the more _______ the manager.
A Inefficient
B Efficient
C Ineffective
D Effective
✅ Correct Answer: A — Inefficient Efficiency = the proportion of resources used in production. If resources are not being used (unused) = the manager is Inefficient. (الـ Efficiency = نسبة الموارد المُستخدمة في الإنتاج. لو الموارد مش بتتاستخدم = المدير Inefficient.)
Difference: Efficiency = using resources correctly · Effectiveness = reaching goals
Question 8 Final 2021 · Q1.28
A manager is considered efficient and effective if the manager _______.
A Does not reach goals and does not waste resources
B Reaches goals and does not waste resources
C Does not reach goals and wastes resources
D Reaches goals and wastes resources
✅ Correct Answer: B — Reaches goals and does not waste resources Efficient = no resource waste · Effective = reaches goals. Both together = Reaches goals AND does not waste resources. (Efficient = ما بيهدرش موارد · Effective = بيوصل للهدف.)
Other options: A = Efficient but Ineffective · C = Inefficient and Ineffective · D = Effective but Inefficient
Question 9 Final 2021 · Q1.29
In any organization, change-related activities include _______.
A Clarifying objectives of jobs in organizations
B Encouraging innovative thinking
C Providing recognition for achievements and contributions
D Consulting when making decisions
E Short-term planning
✅ Correct Answer: B — Encouraging innovative thinking Change-related activities = activities related to modifying organizational components, most importantly innovative thinking. (الـ Change-related activities = الأنشطة المتعلقة بتعديل مكونات المنظمة.)
Others: A, E = Task-related · C, D = People-related
Question 10 Final 2025 · Q59
Which of the following statements about the management function is true?
A It is a one-time function in an organization
B It is a series of continuing and unrelated activities
C It reaches goals by working with and through people
D It involves and concentrates on reaching individual goals
E It does not consider the people aspect of organizations
✅ Correct Answer: C — It reaches goals by working with and through people This is exactly the definition of Management — reaching goals by working with and through people. (ده هو تعريف الـ Management بالظبط.)
Why others are wrong: A = wrong, management is ongoing · B = wrong, activities are interrelated · D = wrong, it achieves organizational not individual goals · E = completely wrong, people are central

Cheat Sheet — Quick Reference (المرجع السريع)

All definitions and tables in one place (كل التعريفات والجداول في مكان واحد)

🏷️ Key Definitions (التعريفات الأساسية)

Management
The process of reaching organizational goals by working with and through people and other organizational resources
Planning
Determining what must be done + how + when — the first and primary function (الوظيفة الأولى والأساسية)
Organizing
Distributing tasks to departments and individuals and creating necessary mechanisms (توزيع المهام على الأقسام والأفراد)
Influencing
Guiding and motivating people = Motivating = Leading = Directing = Actuating
Controlling
Measuring performance + comparing to standards + applying correction if needed (قياس الأداء + مقارنته بالمعايير + تطبيق تصحيح لو لزم)
Effectiveness
Doing the RIGHT things — reaching goals (الوصول للأهداف)
Efficiency
Doing things RIGHT — using resources in the best way without waste (استخدام الموارد بأفضل طريقة بدون إهدار)
Technical Skills
Applying specialized knowledge — Low-level managers
Human Skills
The ability to work with people — Middle-level managers
Conceptual Skills
Seeing the organization as a whole — Top-level managers
4 Org. Resources
Human · Monetary · Raw Materials · Capital (Assets)
